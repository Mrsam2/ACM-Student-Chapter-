ACM Visit - Persistent Systems

ACM Visit 2025

The ACM Student Chapter of the Computer Engineering Department visited Persistent Systems, Nagpur on 15th February 2025. The visit included two expert sessions by Prof. Viraj Kumar (IISc, ACM India) covering "The Impact of GenAI on Programming Courses" for faculty and "Writing Code Effectively with GenAI Tools" for students.

20 students and faculty members Dr. Pallavi Wankhede and Prof. Yogita Nikhare joined, gaining valuable industry exposure and insights.
