Power BI Workshop

December 10, 2024
10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
SVPCET Campus, Computer Lab 3

The Power BI Workshop organized by the ACM student chapter on December 10, 2024, provided participants with a comprehensive introduction to Microsoft Power BI. This hands-on workshop covered essential topics including software installation, data scraping techniques, report generation, and advanced visualization features.

Participants gained practical experience in transforming raw data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards and reports. The workshop was designed to cater to both beginners and those with some prior experience in data analysis.

Workshop Highlights

  • Introduction to Power BI and its applications in business intelligence
  • Connecting to various data sources and data transformation
  • Creating interactive visualizations and dashboards
  • DAX formulas for advanced calculations
  • Sharing and publishing reports to Power BI Service
  • Best practices for effective data storytelling

The session was led by experienced speakers including Manasvi Giradkar, Ayush Kshirsagar, and Paritosh Magare, who ensured an engaging learning environment with dedicated query support throughout the workshop.
