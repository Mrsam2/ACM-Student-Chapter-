ACM Hackathon 2025

Our biggest event of the year! A 24-hour hackathon where teams compete to build innovative solutions to real-world problems

Date April 12-13, 2025
Duration 24 Hours Non-stop
Venue SVPCET Main Campus
Prize Pool ₹50,000

About the Hackathon

ACM Hackathon 2025 is our flagship event that brings together the brightest minds in technology for an intense 24-hour coding marathon. This year's theme focuses on "Technology for Social Good" - challenging participants to develop innovative solutions that can make a positive impact on society.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, this hackathon provides the perfect platform to showcase your skills, learn new technologies, collaborate with talented peers, and compete for exciting prizes while working on meaningful projects.

Connect with like-minded developers, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders
