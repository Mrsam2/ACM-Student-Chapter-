AI & Machine Learning Tech Talk

Join us for an insightful tech talk on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning trends, featuring industry experts

Date February 15, 2025
Time 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Venue Main Auditorium

About the Tech Talk

Join us for an exciting and informative tech talk on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, where industry experts will share their insights on the latest trends, applications, and future predictions in the AI/ML field. This session is designed to inspire and educate students about the transformative power of AI technologies.

Whether you're a beginner curious about AI or an advanced student looking to deepen your understanding, this tech talk will provide valuable insights into the current state and future directions of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Topics to be Covered

Current AI/ML Trends

Explore the latest developments in artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies

Career Opportunities

Discover various career paths and opportunities in the AI and ML industry

Live Project Demos

Watch hands-on demonstrations of real-world AI/ML projects and applications

Interactive Q&A

Engage with experts through an interactive question and answer session

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding of current AI/ML landscape and emerging trends
  • Insights into real-world applications of AI across industries
  • Knowledge about career paths and skill requirements in AI/ML
  • Exposure to cutting-edge research and development in AI
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Guidance on how to start your journey in AI/ML
