Who We Are

The ACM Student Chapter at the Department of Computer Engineering, SVPCET, was established in 2019 to cultivate a vibrant community of technology enthusiasts. Our mission is to provide students with opportunities to enhance their skills, collaborate on innovative projects, and engage with industry leaders.

We organize a variety of events, including seminars, workshops, and technical talks, aimed at bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world application. Our members benefit from networking opportunities, hands-on experience, and insights into the latest advancements in computing.

Through our activities, we aim to empower students to become proficient professionals who can contribute meaningfully to the tech industry and drive technological innovation.
